Warhawk (PS3)

This site was created to commemorate a year since Warhawk's online shutdown. Warhawk went down at 12:00 PM PST on January 31, 2019. The site contains stats that were gathered < 24 hours beforehand.

Only leaderboard stats are currently available. They cover the first ~2004 entries of all sortable columns (22 in total). Columns are sorted in descending order. Data is more detailed than what was shown in Warhawk's menus.
